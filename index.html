2021 Leaders Survey

As we begin our work in seeking the Lord for nominees for elected leadership, we would like to ask you for your help. For a brief description of the elected positions to help you see the leadership roles for which we are providing nominees, click here.

We are filling leadership positions as elders (overseeing the purpose and people of the church), governing board members (overseeing the budget and policies), and deacons (serving the needs of people in concrete ways). We are seeking spiritually discerning leaders who can lead and navigate the cultural complexities of our diverse congregation.

Who have you noticed within our membership who are faithfully serving well? Members who are walking with Jesus in a way that reflects his character as they serve him at work, at home, or within the body of Christ. They could be the person who helps with the cleanup, or leaders of groups, or gifted teachers. Maybe they have a compassionate heart, a discerning spirit, or are servant leaders who you look up to for spiritual guidance; or is there someone you know who is good with numbers like an accountant who you respect as spiritually mature?

Though there are many leaders at Wellspring using their gifts to serve God and others well, the elected positions, required by our bylaws, must be members.
